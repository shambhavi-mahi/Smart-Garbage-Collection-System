♻️ Quick Waste Segregation Guide

Sort it right — protect the city, reduce landfill.

📦 Dry Waste

Paper, cardboard, plastic bottles, cans, glass jars. Keep clean and dry before disposal.

🥕 Wet Waste

Food scraps, vegetable peels, garden waste. Use separate green bins daily.

☢️ Hazardous

Batteries, chemicals, paints, medicines. Never mix with regular waste — special drop-offs only.

💻 E-Waste

Old phones, chargers, laptops, TVs. Drop at certified e-waste collection centres.
